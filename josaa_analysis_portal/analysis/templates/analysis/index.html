JEE Advanced

JEE Advanced is the second stage of the Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) conducted annually for admissions to prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs). It tests students on their understanding of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

For more detailed information and preparation tips, visit the official JEE Advanced website.

Joint Seat Allocation Authority (JoSAA)

JoSAA is responsible for coordinating the seat allocation process for admissions to 23 IITs, 31 NITs, IIEST Shibpur, and 26 other Government Funded Technical Institutes (GFTIs) based on JEE Advanced ranks. It ensures a fair and transparent allocation of seats.

Learn more about JoSAA and its role in the official JoSAA website.
